Sometimes, when Ofqual recognises an awarding organisation, it does so conditionally. In these cases, the organisation has to follow rules specifically set for it. This is usually the case where the organisation is new or has not had a lot of previous experience offering the types of qualification it has applied to offer as regulated qualifications, but it may also happen after an audit or investigation of an organisation. These rules are called 'special conditions'.
